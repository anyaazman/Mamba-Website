01 STRATEGY DEEP DIVE

V1 Growth - Smart Breakeven System

Conservative strategy with 2:1 reward-to-risk ratio. When trade reaches 50% profit, Stop Loss auto-moves to breakeven—eliminating risk while keeping full upside.

V1 Trading Parameters Explained
V1
🎯 Take Profit (TP)

Your profit target per trade. Typical: 30-100 pips. Example: TP 50 = +50 pips profit target.

🛡️ Stop Loss (SL)

Maximum loss per trade. Use 2:1 ratio. Example: TP 50 pips = SL 25 pips.

📊 Break Even (BE)

Auto-moves SL to entry when profit hits this level. Set to 50% of TP. Example: TP 50 = BE 25.

💡 Pro Tip: For optimal V1 Growth results, use TP: 50, SL: 25, BE: 25 on major pairs like EUR/USD during London session.

V2 Superscalping - Dynamic Trailing Stop

High-frequency strategy for active traders. Stop Loss trails price movement at fixed distance—locking profits while letting winners run. Ideal for trending markets.

V2 Superscalping Parameters Explained
V2
⚡ Take Profit (TP)

Quick profit target. Range: 10-30 pips. Lower values = more trades daily.

🛡️ Stop Loss (SL)

Tight stop for scalping precision. Use 2:1 ratio. Example: TP 20 = SL 10.

📊 Break Even (BE)

Trigger after BE activates trailing stop. Set to 50% of TP. Example: TP 20 = BE 10.So your SL will trigger the movement toward the current price.

🎯 BE from Current Price

Trailing distance—how many pips SL stays behind peak price. Lower = tighter protection. Higher = larger potential gains.The distance from current price from SL after BE triggered.

⚡ Pro Tip: For aggressive V2 Superscalping, try TP: 20, SL: 10, BE: 10, BE from Current: 8 on volatile pairs like GBP/JPY during NY session.
