Last updated: 6 July 2026

Please read this statement carefully before using the Mamba Management App. Trading foreign exchange (forex), commodities, and other leveraged derivatives carries a high level of risk and is not suitable for all investors.

1. Risk of Loss

You may sustain a loss of some or all of your invested capital. You should never trade with money you cannot afford to lose. Only risk capital — funds whose loss would not change your standard of living — should be used for trading.

2. Leverage Risk

Leveraged trading magnifies both gains and losses. A relatively small market movement can produce a proportionally larger impact on your deposited funds, and can result in losses that exceed your initial deposit with some brokers and account types.

3. Automated Trading & Technology Risk

The Service relies on software, network connectivity, broker infrastructure, and market data feeds. Failures or delays in any of these — including internet outages, server downtime, platform errors, requotes, or slippage — can cause orders to be executed late, at different prices than expected, or not at all. Stop Loss, Take Profit, breakeven, and trailing mechanisms depend on market conditions and broker execution and are not guaranteed to fill at the intended price, particularly during high volatility or price gaps.

4. No Guarantee of Profit

No trading strategy, indicator, or automation — including the V1 Growth and V2 Superscalping strategies — can guarantee profits or eliminate risk. Any statistics, examples, or case scenarios shown on this Site are illustrations, not promises of future performance.

5. Past Performance

Past performance, whether real or simulated, is not indicative of future results. Market conditions change, and strategies that performed well historically may perform poorly in the future.

Before trading, carefully consider your investment objectives, level of experience, and risk appetite, and seek advice from an independent licensed financial adviser if you have any doubts.
